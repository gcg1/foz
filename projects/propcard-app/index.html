Creating a communication platform for property teams

The Challenge

Early user research indicated that although property teams loved the idea of communication platforms like Slack and Microsoft Teams, the fluctuation of team members between transactions meant pre-existing solutions like Slack and Microsoft teams were falling flat. Our goal was to create a central source of truth — a platform to communicate and coordinate the work around real estate transactions for agents and home owners through to transaction coordinators and brokerage management.
